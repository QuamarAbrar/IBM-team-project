Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a test?

Navigate to "Upcoming Tests" 10 minutes before the scheduled time. A "Join Now" button will appear active. Click it to enter the test environment.

What permissions are required for tests?

You must grant permission for your Camera, Microphone, and Screen Sharing to ensure academic integrity during the monitored session.

Can I retake a test?

Generally, tests cannot be retaken once submitted. However, if you faced a technical error, please contact support immediately.

How is the leaderboard calculated?

Rankings are based on a weighted average of your test scores, accuracy percentage, and the total number of tests attempted.

How do I download my test results?

Go to "Past Test Records", click on any test, and then click the "Download Report" button to get a detailed PDF report.
